WHAT WE DO

They strive to provide nutritious food and support to individuals and families who are struggling financially, facing food shortages, or experiencing crises. Bhutan Food Bank offers healthy fresh and packaged food and beverages that have been donated from local businesses and farms, food drives and individuals.Food is also received through other non-profit organizations.Additional meats, eggs and bulk staples are purchased with donated funds.No one is turned away all are cared for. We provide assistance to all people without regard to religious preference, pregnant woman,age, sex, marital status, or disability, and all services are given at no charge or obligation. The Bhutan Food Bank is also a great place to learn more about additional resources and organizations that connect people to the services they may need throughout the community.
